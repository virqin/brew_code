Help

Use UP and DOWN to scroll vertically and change link selections. Press SELECT to follow a link, or to fill in a form field.

Press LEFT or RIGHT to bring up the toolbar, which has these buttons:

  •   Back takes you to the previous page.
  •   Refresh reloads the current page.
  •   Goto allows you to enter any URL (BREW Browser remembers the last few you've entered).
  •   Info can tell you a little about the current page.
  •   Bookmarks lets you manage your bookmarks.
  •   Help brings you back here.
  •   Options lets you configure BREW Browser.

Have fun!
